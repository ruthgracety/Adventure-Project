Why have adventures?

Adventures are so therapeutic for your body, soul, mind and spirit

Adventure activities can improve your physical health. Spending time outdoors can help you feel energized and refreshed, and exercise can strengthen your brain and memory. Adventure activities can improve your mental health. They can reduce stress, increase attention, and improve your mood. Adventure activities can improve your emotional health. They can help you feel more accomplished, and can give you a sense of fulfillment. Adventure activities can provide a form of escapism from the monotony of everyday life. Adventure activities can provide opportunities to make memories that will last a lifetime.

Some examples of adventure activities include hiking, kayaking, volunteering, exploring a foreign city, cycling, trekking, glacier walking, birding, river rafting, wildlife spotting, snorkeling, caving, scuba diving, tubing, quad biking, and mountain climbing.
